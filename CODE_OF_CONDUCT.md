# Code of Conduct

## Short version

Be kind. Disagree without being a jerk. We're building this in public.

## Less short version

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### Examples of behavior that helps

- Respectful, constructive feedback
- Asking before assuming
- Crediting people
- Showing your work

### Examples of behavior that hurts

- Personal attacks, insults
- Trolling, dismissive comments
- Public shaming
- Posting someone's private info without permission

## Enforcement

Issues with conduct → contact `@josueramosleites-collab` directly or via [pixeldesk.ai/contato](https://pixeldesk.ai/contato).

Maintainers can remove comments, lock threads, or ban repeat offenders without warning if needed.
